Set Amount for OT and Penalties

By default, overtime and penalties are calculated based on the hourly salary. (The hourly salary is calculated by dividing the monthly salary by the number of days in a month and the shift hours.) If you set a "SET" amount, overtime and penalties will be calculated based on that amount.

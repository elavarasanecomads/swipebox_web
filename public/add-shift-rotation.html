Daily Shift Rotation

In a Day Wise rotation, employees switch shifts on a daily basis. This means that each worker experiences a different shift each day of the week.

Weekly Shift Rotation

Week Wise rotation involves employees changing shifts on a weekly basis. Each worker would have a consistent shift for an entire week before transitioning to a different one the following week.

Bi-Weekly Shift Rotation

Week Wise rotation involves employees changing shifts on a weekly basis. Each worker would have a consistent shift for an entire week before transitioning to a different one the following week.

Monthly Shift Rotation

In Month Wise rotation, employees switch shifts monthly, ensuring that each worker maintains a steady schedule for an entire month before transitioning to a different one in the subsequent month.m

Hybrid Shift Rotation

In a Hybrid Shift Rotation, there is a combination of both daily and weekly rotations. This type of rotation offers a balance between the frequency of shift changes, providing variety on a daily basis and consistency on a weekly basis.
